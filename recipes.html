RECIPES

FAWORKI

Faworki, also known as Crust or Angel Wings or many other names, is a delightful, crispy light dough snack, which has been deeply friend in oil and topped with sweet powdered sugar. The time to prepare these takes about 10 minutes. To cook, about 25 minutes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 400g (2cups) of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 4 egg yokes
  • 3-5 tablespoons of sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla or citrus zest (optional)
  • lard/oil for frying (2L)
  • powdered suagr

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine flour, sugar & salt in a relatively big bowl
  2. In a seperate bowl, combine the eggs, sour cream, and vanilla or citrus zest if present
  3. Add both ingredients together into the big bowl and stir until the fough comes together, add more sour cream if necessary. Milk will work also
  4. Place dough on the table and roll out to a very thin state
  5. Cut the dough into rectangular pieces, around 1x3 inches or so, and also a small whole in the center
  6. Pull one end of the rectangle through the slit and twist
  7. Heat lard/oil to approx 160-180 degrees Celsius
  8. Fry in batches so that each has its own space, approx. 7-12 each time
  9. Take out when cripsy golden and place onto paper towels and leave to dry for about 30 seconds - 1 minute
  10. Place in a bowl and add powdered sugar on top
