ABOUT US

Sweet Dreams originally started in Warsaw, Poland but due to a lot of competition in the city and the rest of the country we decided to relocate. We picked Galway as our new starting point as we heard it is a good place to start our business because where there isn’t any Central-European big pastry nor candy companies located here. Knowing about a lot of our fellow Eastern Europeans citizens migrated to Ireland we knew they will help us to expand the business since everyone will miss their own country tastes.

Our pastries and candies are only Eastern European related which we hope will attract the locals to give a try and help us expand even more. We plan to expand across Ireland to open up more of our shops in other cities and soon maybe even in the UK. We advise you to try them out for yourself and let us know your feedback As a company we promise to deliver fresh products for every citizen in Galway City to taste which will not dissapoint.

- Sweet Dreams team
